About me

I am an evolutionary biologist born and raised in the beautifully chaotic Mexico City. I have over ten years of experience in molecular and computational biology, and my main research interest is to understand the biological and evolutionary mechanisms between immunity and aging. For this, I study non-model organisms, with bats being my main "non-model model organism". But not all in life is research. I am deeply committed to building community and to scientific outreach. In this sense, I am a cofounder of an organization named Mujeres Latinas in STEM and a cofounder of a scientific outreach project named Ciencia Pal Barrio. When I am not in the lab or doing science communication, you will definitely find me making new friends, dancing, or traveling somewhere in the world.
